Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s:
1. Use privacy settings to protect your account  
2. Share information responsibly  
3. Be respectful in online communication  
4. Think before posting anything  
5. Keep your profiles professional  

Don’ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information  
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
4. Avoid engaging in online arguments  
5. Do not trust unverified sources
