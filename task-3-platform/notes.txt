Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms

--- Coding Platform: HackerRank ---
Challenge Completed: Solve Me First
Difficulty: Beginner
Task: Write a program that reads two integers and outputs their sum.

Even though the problem was simple, it was a really good introduction to how competitive coding platforms work, how you submit solutions, and how automated test cases evaluate your code. A genuinely useful first step for someone just starting out with programming.

--- Collaboration Tool: Google Forms ---
Form Created: Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz
Number of Questions: 5
Topics Covered: Digital literacy basics and online safety

All responses are automatically collected in a linked Google Sheet. I had used Google Forms before for casual stuff but this was the first time I actually paid attention to how the backend and response collection works, and it was honestly really impressive to see how well it integrates with Google Sheets.
