Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Tool Used: Canva
Link:https://canva.link/8uo1d8ozrm573h7

For this task I created a one page infographic on digital literacy using Canva.
The infographic covers three main topics:

1. What digital literacy is and why it matters for students
2. Safe internet practices like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links
3. Email etiquette including proper subject lines, greetings and professional language

I chose Canva because its beginner friendly and has a lot of ready made templates which made the designing process much easier for me. The biggest challenge was keeping the content concise while also making the layout look clean and readable at the same time.
