Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks  
4. Use official apps and avoid third-party links  
5. Check payment requests carefully before approving  
6. Enable security features like app lock and two-factor authentication  
7. Regularly monitor your bank transactions  
8. Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call the helpline number 1930.
